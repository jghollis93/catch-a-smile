Catch A Smile

Open to all — we welcome people with disabilities & special needs to enjoy fishing & the great outdoors.

A friendly, fully accessible fishery in Gloucester for families, schools and organisations. Fishing parties catered for.

Book a Lesson Learn More

New to fishing? You're exactly who we're here for. 🐟

We warmly encourage anyone who has never fished — or only fished a handful of times — to book and get a lesson. Our welcoming team will help you every step of the way.

Welcome

Fishing & outdoor space for everyone

Catch A Smile is open to all. We welcome people with disabilities and special needs to enjoy fishing and the outdoors. Family fishing parties catered for, and schools and organisations are always welcome.

£10.00

per person — includes all equipment & bait
